Launch: Subscription Hike Watchlist

Built a local-first tracker for subscriptions with two goals: (1) see your true monthly/annual spend, and (2) stop getting surprised by renewals and price increases.

What works

  • Add subscriptions with amount, cycle, and next charge date.
  • See monthly + annual totals.
  • See charges in the next 14 days.
  • Track price hikes as simple history entries.

Next

  • Import from bank CSV.
  • Calendar export (.ics) for renewals.
  • Category breakdown + charts.
